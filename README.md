## PROJECT OVERVIEW

This repository contains the tasks completed as part of the Graphic Design Internship offered by Oasis Infobyte.

A fictional gaming cafe named "Rock Riley Gaming Cafe" was used as the brand identity across all tasks to maintain consistency in design, branding, and presentation.

## COMPLETED TASKS

### Task 1 - Poster Design

Designed a promotional poster for the grand opening of Rock Riley Gaming Cafe.

Features:

Free gaming for the first hour on opening day
50% discount on snacks during the first week
Gaming-themed visual design
Promotional marketing layout

### Task 2 - Logo Design

Created a neon-inspired logo representing the identity of Rock Riley Gaming Cafe.

Features:

Modern gaming aesthetic
Minimal branding approach
Suitable for digital and print usage

### Task 3 - Business Card Design

Designed a professional business card for Rock Riley Gaming Cafe.

Features:

Brand logo placement
Contact information
Consistent visual identity
Gaming-themed styling

### Task 4 - Infographic Design

Created an infographic explaining how cloud gaming can reduce costs and improve scalability for gaming cafes.

Topics Covered:

Reduced hardware costs
Lower maintenance requirements
Scalability benefits
Operational advantages of cloud gaming solutions
